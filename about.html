About Us

Who We Are

Doha Project, established in April 2000

Doha Project has now grown to be a well-established , highly reputed organization and most respected names in the field of oil and gas industry in the Middle East region. We are committed to serve our respective clients in the Qatar market by providing a complete range of products, accessories and services for oil and gas related projects.

Doha Project Co. continuously improving upon all aspects of our company activities, and this is proven by our Clients within the Energy Sector entrusting us with their business requirements.

As an ISO certified organization, our dedicated team ensures each Client is provided with cost-effective and highest quality service, especially Oil and Gas companies require a high level of safety and security in the workplace for carrying out their day-to-day functions smoothly. At Doha Project, we measure our success by customer delight. The company product range is extensive which include pipes, pipe fittings, oil field items, safety and structural steel all within the quickest possible turnaround time.

In a short span of eight years, the company has established excellent business tie-ups with international suppliers /manufacturers of the specialized materials in the Qatar and Worldwide. We grow through our alliance with customers. And we guarantee quality products at competitive prices.
