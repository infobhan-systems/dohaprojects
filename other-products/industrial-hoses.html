Industrial Hoses

At DP, we supply different type of hoses from a wide range of the quality manufacturer. We can provide any types of hoses which are used in oil & gas and other allied industries. Simply, we have the right Principals to meet your special requirements and quality standards.

Types of hoses:
  1. Industrial Braided PTFE Hoses.
  2. Braided Metal Hose
  3. Silicone hose
  4. Rubber/Thermoplastic hoses
  5. Heated hoses
  6. Racing Hose
  7. Fire sleeve/Abrasion
  8. Formed Hose

Design temperature: -146 ºC to 500ºC and above
Design Pressure: 0-1000 PSI and above
End connection: Rubber seal, Ferrule, Tail, Fixed, Floating flange, Cam lock, Swivel Flange, nipples &as per client requirements
Materials: 304,316,310, 321, 347, Inconel, & Special materials as per your request.

Any Special & Exotic materials on request
