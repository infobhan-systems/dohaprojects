Instrumentation Fittings

DP supplies a wide selection of tube fittings. Our high pressure tube fittings come in 316 Stainless Steel, Duplex, Super Duplex SS, Hastelloy, Inconel, Monel& other special grades available on request.

Materials & Specifications
  • Standard Grades: 316 Stainless Steel, Duplex, Super Duplex SS, Hastelloy, Inconel, Monel and special grades available on request
  • Types:Elbows, Straight Connectors, Unions, Tees, Crosses, Adapters and more
  • Sizes: 1/16” to 2” OD
  • Working Pressures: (High Pressure) 10000 & 15000 psig, as well as General Instrumentation Fittings (psig varies depending on size)

Any Special & Exotic materials on request
