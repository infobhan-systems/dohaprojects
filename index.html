About

Doha Project

Doha Project Trading & Services W.L.L., established in April 2000 has now grown to be a well-established, highly reputed and most respected organization, inheriting credibility and reliability in the field of Oil and Gas industry in the Middle East region. We are committed to serving our clients by providing a wide range of diverse products, accessories and services for the Oil & Gas industry and infrastructure related projects. Our product range is extensive which includes the supply of pipes & fittings, Flanges, valves, gaskets, safety and security products, structural steel sections & plates, packaging bags etc.

We are experts in the sourcing and supplying of materials to meet the individual requirements of a project, regardless of size, scope or difficulty. We have the right team aware of the importance of on-time delivery and the pressures of meeting project schedules to satisfy our client’s requirement. Over the years, the company has established excellent business tie-ups for the supply of specialized materials/equipment with international suppliers/ manufacturers in the region. We grow and develop formidable, strong & long business relationship through our dedication and alliance with all our customers.
