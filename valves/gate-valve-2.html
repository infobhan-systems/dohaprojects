Gate Valves

At DP, we stock & supply different type of gate valves from reputed manufacturers. We can provide you valves of ASTM, ASME, ANSI, API, and MSS. which you need to complete your project & maintenance on time and on budget.

  • Material Grades: Carbon, Stainless, Chrome Moly, Low Temperature carbon steel, Duplex and super duplex and other exotic material grades.
  • Types: Cryogenic, Integral Flanged, Bellows Sealed, Extended Body Gate, Y-Pattern, and Pressure Seal.
  • Sizes: 1/2” to 48”
  • Pressure Rating: 150, 300, 600, 800, 900, 1500, 1690, 2500, 2680, and 4500#
  • End Configurations: Threaded, Socket Weld, Butt Weld, Plain End, Raised Face Flange, Ring Type Joint Flange, and Flat Face.
  • Trim Materials: 410, 304, 316, 317, 321, 347, Alloy 20, Monel 400, Monel 500, Stellite and other exotic material grades.
  • Industry Standards: ASTM, ASME, ANSI, API, JIS and MSS
